Get To Know Me

Welcome to my website I personally coded!

My name is Jasmine Cisneros and my work displays an art variety from traditional to digital art. With influences from my previous community college and with my university, my main medium seem to have change over the course of time. In this portfolio I use charcoal to truly bring out the realism I like to capture in my pieces. When it comes to art style I like to try and bring out every detail I can get. However when I arrived to university I began to develop more on my coding skills. I want to experiment and create with art. Ever since I was young art was always a big passion of mine, I knew that when I got older I wanted to explore the other medium possibilities. Art to me is trying out different mediums to keep my brain creativity overflowing with new perspectives.
